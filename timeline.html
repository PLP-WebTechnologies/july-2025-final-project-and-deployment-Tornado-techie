🗓️ Our Services Timeline

Day 0

Customer submits request through the website form.

Day 1

Automated acknowledgement email sent to customer.

Day 2–3

Consultant reviews request and contacts customer to schedule a call.

Day 3–5

Introductory call held (30–45 minutes) to understand needs.

Day 7–14

Site visit or detailed consultation scheduled if required.

Day 10–19

Consultant sends tailored proposal with next steps, timelines, and costs.
